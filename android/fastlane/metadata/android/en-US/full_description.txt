A secure, open-source Matrix messenger for Ubuntu Touch, Android and iOS.

Open
Opensource and open development where everyone can join.

Nonprofit
Community funded and developed.

Clean Design ♥
Modern design with many theme settings including a dark mode. 

One-to-one and group chats
Unlimited groups and direct chats.

Easy
Made as simple to use as possible.

Free
Free to use for everyone without ads.

Decentralized
No central server you're forced to use. Use the server you trust or host your own.

Compatible
Compatible with Element, Fractal, Nheko and all Matrix messengers.


Built with a vision

Imagine a world where everyone can choose the messenger they like and is still able to chat with all of their friends.
A world where there are no companies spying on you when you send messages to loved ones.
A world where apps prioritize privacy and user freedom over profit. ♥
